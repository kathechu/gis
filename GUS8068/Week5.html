United States Electric Power Generation

Map of Biomass, Geothermal, and Solar Power Plants

Map authored by Katherine Chu

Each energy source's visibility can be toggled using the layer control. Power is in units of megawatts (MW).

Design Principle and Change in Map

For the map, I followed the principle: Use relative units and breakpoints. While the original map already follows this principle, I felt that it needed an extra step for it to be easily understood by viewers. By adding a legend for the marker sizes, the viewer can better understand the capacity of the different plants at a glance.
